Tag Subscription Feature

Murmur gives users the ability to receive their desired content in their inbox through the tag subscription settings in every mailing list. The demo showcs an example of tags in a group (e.g. EECS mailing list) which can be configured in the settings as seen on the left. These personal tag preferences result in the filtering of emails that enter your inbox as seen on the right.
